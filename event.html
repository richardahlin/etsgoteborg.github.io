ETS Göteborg is taking place on June 13-14 2026 at Kviberg Park. It is modern sports facility close to central Gothenburg. On Saturday evening there will be an afterparty 🥳

  • Played with Premier Spike balls and nets
  • Shoes with cleats are allowed

In Sweden, alcohol is only sold at Systembolaget. On Saturdays they typically close at 15:00 and on Sundays they are closed.

It is recommended to check your local store's hours in advance.
