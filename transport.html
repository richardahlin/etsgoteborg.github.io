Transport

Venue address: Krutvägen 2, 415 28 Göteborg

Public Transport

Getting around Gothenburg by public transport is easy. Plan your journey and buy tickets in the app Västtrafik To Go. Tickets are available for single trips, 24 hours, and 72 hours.

Trams serve the venue from more parts of the city and run more frequently than the bus lines — they're the recommended option.

Getting to Gothenburg

By Plane

Fly to Landvetter Airport and take the bus to the city centre — Flygbussarna and FlixBus both serve the route.

Alternatively, fly to Copenhagen Airport and continue by train directly from the airport's train station — see By Train below.

By Train

Take the train to Gothenburg Central Station.

Trains from across Sweden are operated by SJ. For the Stockholm–Gothenburg route, Vy is another alternative.

Travelling via Denmark?

Öresundståget runs directly from Copenhagen Central Station and Copenhagen Airport to Gothenburg. Tickets are cheapest in the app Skånetrafiken — group discount applies when the same person buys two or more tickets.

By Car

If you arrive by car, you can park at the venue.

Parking is easiest managed through one of these apps: Parkering Göteborg, EasyPark, or Parkster.

Parking

Parking is available on site in the middle of Kviberg Park at two parking lots.
