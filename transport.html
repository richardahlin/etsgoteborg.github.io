Transport

Public Transport

You can get around easily with tram and bus in Gothenburg. Search your journey and buy your ticket through the app Västtrafik To Go .

Tickets are available for one-way, 24 hours and 72 hours. The trams to the venue go more frequently and from more areas in the city than the two bus lines.

Tram

Tram lines to venue: 6 7 11
Tram stop: Kviberg.
10 minutes to walk to the venue.

Bus

Bus lines to venue: 56 510
Bus stop: Kvibergsskolan.
5 minutes to walk to the venue.
